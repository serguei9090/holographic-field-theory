## 🚀 1. What We Are Doing
We are developing and benchmarking **CHFT (Complex Holographic Field Theory)**, an alternative non-attention-based language modeling paradigm. It uses:
* **Holographic Reduced Representations (HRR)**: Projecting tokens into complex phase vectors (binding & superposition).
* **Modern Hopfield Memory**: Storing and retrieving key-value patterns iteratively (multi-hop associative recall) as a replacement for standard causal self-attention.

We are testing this on a controlled subset of **TinyStories** (5,000 stories, context length 64, vocabulary size 8,875 tokens) to compare parameter-for-parameter scaling and computational efficiency.
